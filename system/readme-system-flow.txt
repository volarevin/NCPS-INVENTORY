 Web-Based Appointment Scheduler for Nasugbu Computer Parts & Services


Entities:

Admin
Receptionist
Technician
Customer

Expected functions for each entity's dashboard:

I. Admin
a. Home/Dashboard:
-Activity overview 
-Quick useful widgets (Total appointments, Services in progress, completed, active technicians and registered customers).
- Graphs (This month's appointment trends, service type distributions)
-Quick stats about customer satisfaction, monthly revenue, etc.
b. Manage Services:
-View/Add/edit/delete  services
c. Appointments:
-can cancel customer's appointment with reason
-View/edit appointment details
-Add/delete upcoming, in progress, completed, cancelled appointments
-approve/reject appointment
d. Technicians:
-Manage technicians
-quick widgets for available, on job, offline technician and total jobs completed
-View technicians details such as (name, phone nuimber, email, specialty, availability, rating & feedback, appointment history, activity log)
-Remove technician
e. User Account:
-Quick widgets (Total Users, Customers, Staff Members, Total Activities)
-Search user (by name, username, email or phone)
-view users details such as roles, phone number, email, activity logs
-promote/demote customer and staff
-add/remove user
f. Reports:
-quick widgets such as total appointments, total revenue, total customers, sucess rate
-view different visual reports about appointments, services, customer, staff, revenue and trends:
1. appointments
	-view total appointments, completed, pending,confirmed,cancelled
	-view monthly appointment trend graph and appointment status distribution
2. services
	-view total services, most/least requested services
	-view number of bookings by service category graph
	-view revenue by service type
3. customers
	-view total customers, new customers
	-view new customers registration by month
	-view top customers
4. Technicians
	-view technician performance
	-view technician ratings, and total handled
5. revenue
	-view total revenue, avg per appointment, total paid
	-view monthly revenue trend
6. trends
	-view peak booking hours/days
	-view cancellation reason by category


II. Receptionist
a. Dashboard
-quick widgets for total pending, in progress, confirmed, completed appointments
-view all today's and upcoming appointments
-view service summary by category
b. Appointments
-can cancel customer's appointment with reason
-view total, pending, in progress, confirmed, completed, cancelled appointments
-search appointments
-update appointment details
-approve/reject appointments
-delete appointments
-add walk-in appointmentst
c. My account/profile
-edit/update account info

III. Technician
a. Dashboard
--quick widgets of total assigned, pending, in progress completed appointments
--view today & upcoming own appointment and mark them as in progress, completed and cancelled
b. Appointments
-can cancel customer's appointment with reason
--view/edit all appointments and history
c. My account/Profile
-View own ratings and feedbacks
-edit/update account info
-delete account

IV. Customer
a. Dashboard
-view simple appointment statistics
-view next appointment details and appointment updates
b. Appointments
-create, view, cancel(with reason), edit and update appointment
-rate technician by completed appointment and send feedback
-view technician details for the upcoming appointment to allow them to contact
-view appointment history
c. My account/profile
-edit/update account info
-delete account
